# Privacy Notes

This app stores improvement ideas, timer state, and reward unlock status on the device using Capacitor Preferences. The app does not send entered business content to an external server.

Google AdMob is used to display banner and rewarded ads. AdMob may collect identifiers and usage data for advertising, measurement, fraud prevention, and service operation. If tracking permission is denied on iOS, the app requests non-personalized ads.

Before App Store submission, publish a public privacy policy URL that includes the AdMob data-use disclosure and configure App Privacy labels in App Store Connect.
